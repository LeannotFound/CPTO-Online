BASUD





Basud is the town with a complete package to offer when it comes to tourism - from beaches, mountains, rivers, falls, and farms to historical landmarks and faith tourism. Basud has a lot for tourists to experience. It also has a 14-year old festival named Rahugan festival which is celebrated at the same time as the feast of Saint Raphael the Archangel Parish Church, which is every October 24.
